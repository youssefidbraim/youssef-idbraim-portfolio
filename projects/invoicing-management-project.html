Invoicing Management Project

A web application that allows you to manage invoices,this application offers you several functionalities to create sales invoices, quotes, invoice receipts, electronic invoices (e-invoices), invoices with and without VAT, a customer and product database, etc.

Invoicing is an inseparable step of the administrative aspect of your activity. It is imperative to provide your clients with an invoice that complies with the law, in exchange for the payments they make. This supporting document contains several pieces of information about you, your customers, as well as your services.
